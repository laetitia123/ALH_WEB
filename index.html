About Us

African Leaders Hub is a Non-Profit Organization established in Rwanda with a vision to develop 3,000,000 African Leader throughout Africa by 2035. We are a community driven organization that puts communitiesâ€™ empowerment at the forefront of our programs and ensure they receive what they need to become effective community leaders. African Leaders Hub is in the registration process, as a Rwandan Non-Profit Organization, and we are envisioning to expand our programs to Burundi, Uganda, Tanzania and Kenya by 2025 and other parts of Africa by 2030. learn More

Our Services

Community Leaders Program

The future of Rwanda and Africa can only be securely planned by developing future leaders today.

Accountable Leaders Talks

The Accountable Leaders Talks are discussions that take place a monthly basis and will involve politicians, church leaders, business leaders, investors and other individuals who, in different ways, serve Rwandans.

Stop Violence & Unwanted Pregnancies

We fight against domestic, child abuse as well as sexual violence through outreach and campaigns in schools and communities.

Human Rights Outreach
